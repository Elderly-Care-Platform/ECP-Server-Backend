WE NEED YOUR HELP
We are just beginning and we really need your help in building our community together with us! This is how you could make this site more useful, interesting and enjoyable for yourself and for others:
Please fill in your profile. Just 15 minutes of your time will help us know you better and learn from your experience.
  • Please write your personal stories of caregiving and ageing. Also please write about seniors you love.
  • Please share useful tips, experiences, and advice. Or let us know what you are going through today, just the way you share your status on Facebook.
  • Please don't hesitate to ask any question related to ageing or caregiving, and please help others by answering their questions.
We would like to improve, so please let us know your feedback:
  • Have you found the information you were looking for?
  • Was it easy to find this information?
  • What would you like us to change?
  • What would you like us to improve?
  • In general, what aspects of the site do you like and what do you dislike?
Please, suggest:
  • What features would you like us to introduce?
  • What services would you like us to provide to our community in future - both on line and in real life?
  • What products you believe we should sell or lease, to improve lives of our community members?
  • Is there anything else you would like us to do for you?
Are you a (retired or an active) content writer, PR, media-, or marketing specialist, a doctor, or a nurse? Are you passionate and knowledgeable about any aspect of elder care and ageing? Maybe you could help our site with your skills and experience.
Also please spread the word about us within your social networks - off line and on Facebook !
Could you please let us know of any relevant service, product, or senior living facility, which you would like us to list or highlight?
If you are a professional or an institution (for profit or an NGO), please register with us, to offer your services and meet your potential customers here. We would also love to hear from you about collaboration and partnership opportunities.
