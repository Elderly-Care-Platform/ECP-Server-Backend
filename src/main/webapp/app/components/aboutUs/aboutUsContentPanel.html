Improving lives of senior people and of those who care for them.
India is one of the best places in the world to age gracefully, as those younger have always looked after the older here. Yet, a lack of information and of professional support leaves families to deal with issues of senior care on their own.

Not anymore!

We at BeautiifulYears.com are building on-line communities of people taking care of ageing parents with similar conditions (such as Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, and others). Here you can ask any question, and get answers from our experts, as well as advice and support - from people like you, in situations like yours.

People who are taking care of seniors are often not young themselves, and are facing many questions as they age.

How to age gracefully? How to take better care of elders – and how to avoid being taken care of, when we ourselves age? What are the challenges of growing old and its stigmas?

These are some of the issues we would like to discuss – with your help; we hope you will share your experiences with us.

We have just opened an e-shop for life-improving senior care products. In addition to locally manufactured products, we are going to import advanced high-tech products from overseas.

As for services, we do not provide services of our own; instead, we list providers of caregivers, physiotherapists, home diagnostics services, medical equipment suppliers, as well as senior living facilities. Our team verifies all services, talking to their owners and visiting some of them. Most importantly, people who availed of those services can provide their honest feedback on our site.

We are now launching in Beta version, with an aim to improve lives of senior people and of those who take care of them, by:

  • creating communities for mutual support,
  • bringing life-improving products, and
  • Providing socially reviewed listings of senior care services and senior living facilities.
If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to ask our experts anything about senior care services or products!

Please come and visit us often – we are growing our directories and our product catalogs, trying to get better every week.

May all senior people and their families enjoy many beautiful years together!
